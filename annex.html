Tables for forward model#

The tables listed here are part of the forward model used in the multi parameter retrieval. The following Tables 7, 8, 9, 10 are reprinted from [Wentz and Meissner, 2000] with the corresponding formulae are described in Forward Model.

Note

For completeness, the tables also include the coefficients for 23.8 GHz and 89 GHz, which are not available on the CIMR satellite. These coefficients are not used in the retrieval.
